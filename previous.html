The General Assembly (GA) is an event that takes place twice a year, where rectors from the ten universities that make up the alliance meet to make strategic decisions regarding the work to be developed.

About EUNICE

EUNICE, the European University for Customised Education, is a transnational alliance of public universities founded in 2020 and currently present in ten countries: Belgium, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Italy, Poland, Portugal, Spain and Sweden.

General Assembly

EUNICE’s General Assemblies are transnational meetings to which all of the alliance’s members are invited. New projects are proposed and discussed for upcoming years. Rectors, Presidents and other representatives of EUNICE universities have the opportunity to meet onsite.
