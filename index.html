EUNICE

EUNICE'S general assemblies, held twice a year, serve as a key meeting where representatives from all partner universities come together to review progress, make strategic decisions and discuss ongoing initiatives. These general assemblies are critical to maintaining the alliance's cohesion and ensuring that all partners are actively involved in achieving collective goals.

Viseu

Viseu is a city central interior of Portugal. As well is being known for is incredible Dão wines, Viseu is also Known for it's historic route, leaving the city with several historic buildings to visit. For the third year running, Viseu was considered the Portuguese City with the best quality of life.

IPV

The Instituto Politécnico de Viseu (IPV) is a higher education institution located in Viseu, Portugal. Established in 1979, it is one of the country's key politechnic institutes, providing a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various fields.
