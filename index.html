EUNICE

EUNICE'S general assemblies, held twice a year, serve as a key meeting where representatives from all partner universities come together to review progress, make strategic decisions and discuss ongoing initiatives. These general assemblies are critical to maintaining the alliance's cohesion and ensuring that all partners are actively involved in achieving collective goals.
